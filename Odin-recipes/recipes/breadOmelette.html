Bread and Omelette

Only takes a few ingredients to make one of the best Bread and Omelette

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4th onion, diced
  • Oil to fry the egg and bread
  • 2 bread
  • 1 small sliced green onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions to make

  1. First crack the egg into bowl and add in the diced onion , chilli , salt and pepper and mix it well
  2. Next fry the egg and place one of the bread on top of the egg that is being fried
  3. Flip the bread and the egg and place another bread on top of the flipped egg
  4. After a few minutes take the it out from the pan
  5. Voila it is now ready to serve
  6. If needed you can mayo or ketchup to the bread.
