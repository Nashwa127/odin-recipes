Mac-n-Cheese

An indulging and scrumptious Mac-n-cheese with chicken recipe , that will make you bonkers for a second round!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cup pasta
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Paprika , Chilli powder , Oregano
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour and 2 cup Milk
  • Cheese

Directions to make

  1. Cut chicken breasts into strips about 3 inches long.
  2. Marinate the chicken and fry the chicken pieces after 20 minutes.And shred it into small pieces
  3. Boil the pasta
  4. In another pan start by adding 2 tablespoons of butter , stir it.
  5. Then add the flour and gradually add the milk
  6. Add all the spices listed
  7. In the end add the boiled pasta and top it off with some cheese and add the chicken pieces
  8. And voila you have it ready!
